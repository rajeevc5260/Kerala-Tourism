Resorts

Lined by coconut trees and backwaters just lying next to the backwaters, Kerala beaches are on of the top attractions of the state. Beach resorts to enjoy a beach holiday in this gorgeous destination

Art

The traditional paintings of Kerala are a fine art of skill and creative excellence. Most of the works of Kerala are shown here. They bear the stamp of uniqueness in techniques used and aesthetics.

Tradition

Kerala has around 50 forms of traditional dances. Theyyam, Thiruvathirakali, Chakyar Koothu Koodiyattam, and Ottamthullal are some of the prominent dances.Explore about the unique classical dance forms of Kerala.

Food Cusine

The cuisine of Kerala is known for its generous use of spices, coconuts and vegetables exclusively grown in the region. The variety of flavours in this cuisine range from spicy to mellow and sweet.

Activites

Kerala offers some of the most diverse kinds of adventure sports in India that are a must try for an adventure enthusiast, like Parasailing, Trekking, Tree House Stay, Kayaking, Wildlife Safari, Mountain Climbing
